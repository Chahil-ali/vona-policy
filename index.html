Vona — Privacy Policy

Voice-first AI journal by A&S Dev Studio

Effective date: July 4, 2026

In short

  • Your journal lives only on your phone. We have no accounts, no user database, and no access to your entries.
  • Entry text and voice recordings are sent to an AI service only to be transcribed and analyzed, then the results come straight back to your phone.
  • We never sell your data and the app shows no ads.

1. Who we are

Vona is a voice-first journaling app published by A&S Dev Studio. For any question about this policy or any problem with the app, contact us at alichahil047@gmail.com.

2. Data stored on your device

Everything you create in Vona is stored locally on your phone and nowhere else:

  • Journal entries (text, titles, summaries, moods, tags)
  • Voice recordings and photos you attach
  • Places you pin to entries and your categories
  • Your app-lock PIN, kept in your phone's encrypted secure storage

We cannot see, read, or recover any of this. If you uninstall the app without making a backup, this data is permanently gone.

3. Data processed by AI services

Vona's smart features need to process some content outside your phone, through our secure relay server:

  • Voice transcription: your audio recording is sent to the AI provider (Groq, using the Whisper model) to be converted into text.
  • Entry analysis, chat and weekly review: the text of your entries is sent to the AI provider to generate titles, summaries, moods, answers and reviews.

This content is used only to produce the response returned to your phone. We do not store your entries or audio on our server. The AI provider processes the data as described in its own privacy policy and does not use it to train models. Requests travel encrypted (HTTPS).

4. Anonymous identifier

The app generates a random anonymous ID on your device (for example u_abc123…). It contains no personal information — no name, no email, no phone number. We use it only to measure fair usage of the AI features and to link your subscription to your device.

5. Subscriptions and payments

Vona Premium is sold through Google Play (and the Apple App Store on iOS). Payments are handled entirely by the store — we never see your payment details. Purchase validation is processed by RevenueCat using only the anonymous ID described above.

6. Permissions the app asks for

  • Microphone — to record your voice entries. Optional: you can always type instead.
  • Photos — to attach pictures to your entries.
  • Location — to show your position on the map and let you pin a place to an entry. Optional.
  • Notifications — only if you enable the daily journaling reminder.
  • Biometrics (Face ID / fingerprint) — only if you enable the app lock. Biometric data never leaves your phone; it is handled by the operating system.

Every permission is optional and the app keeps working if you refuse it.

7. Backups

You can export a backup file containing your whole journal (including photos and audio). This file is created on your device and you choose where to send or store it. Protect it like you would protect your diary — anyone with the file can read its contents.

8. Deleting your data

  • Deleted entries move to Recently Deleted and are erased forever after 30 days (or immediately if you choose "Delete forever"), including their photo and audio files.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all app data from your phone.

9. Children

Vona is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

10. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update this page and the effective date above. Significant changes will be mentioned in the app's update notes.

11. Contact & support

For app problems, questions, or anything about your privacy, contact us at alichahil047@gmail.com. We answer as fast as we can.
